== COLORNEWS  ==
ColorNews is a perfect free responsive news style WordPress theme. This theme provides beautiful well-organized magazine look and feel, carefully designed to give stunning impression to your visitors. Represent your categories with different colors and make your site colorful and crisp. This theme is specially crafted for news, newspaper, publishing and magazine types of site. Got a new magazine site project? This theme will be an ideal fit.
